Nelson Mandela

The man who saved Africans

Nelson Mandela was a an African foremost nationalist during his life time.Infact, it is not an overstatement that he was one of the best and most successful African presidents.

On 10 May 1994 he was inaugurated as South Africa’s first democratically elected President. On his 80th birthday in 1998 he married Graça Machel, his third wife.

Apart from being a president, he was a freedom fighter, phillantropist and philosopher.
